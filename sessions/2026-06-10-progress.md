## Goals (from `site-overview.md` + `sections.md`)

### Business goals
- Show services and pricing immediately — no "contact for pricing" walls
- Drive one action: contact or book
- Build trust fast with quality photos and clean design
- Rank locally on Google for Bakersfield auto detailing searches

### User flow target
> Landing → skim services & pricing → see proof of quality → contact or book

### Design principles
- Pricing visible without extra clicks
- Mobile-optimized throughout
- Fast load — no bloat
- Every section earns its place
