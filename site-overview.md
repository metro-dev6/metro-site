# Metro Auto Detailing — Site Overview

## Goals
- Show services and pricing immediately — visitors skim, don't read
- Build trust fast — quality photos, clean design, real copy
- Drive one action: contact or book
- Rank locally on Google for Bakersfield auto detailing searches

## Page Structure
Three pages total:
1. Home
2. Services
3. Build Your Estimate (contact/booking form)

## User Flow
Landing → skim services and pricing → see proof of quality → contact or book

## Key Principles
- Pricing visible without clicking — no "contact for pricing" walls
- Mobile optimized throughout
- Fast load times — no bloat
- Every section has a purpose, nothing decorative only
